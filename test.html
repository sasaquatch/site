Favourites

toronto adding more soon

Imanishi

I want more endless nights at this place. Perfect izakaya food every time, and the dai dai orange sour is amazing.

Ramen Isshin

The Tan Tan Ramen makes me forget where I am for 20 minutes. It's a powerful magical potion that will keep you alive year-round.

Dumpling House

Not sure why I always think of ordering dumplings on a Tuesday (they're closed on Tuesdays). Get the juciest fried pork and chives and forget everything that's wrong with the world with the first bite.

The island         

No cell reception in some parts and if you're facing south then it feels like you're in a different country. I never understood the haters. The sound of the waves and the sunsets are a great vibe. It's next level if you bike there.

North of Brooklyn

This place makes NY‚Äîstyle slices and is unpretentious about it. I like to walk around the area for an extra layer of comfy sensations.

Poetry

When returning from a trip, it's always good to reset at a place that has great live jazz and an atmosphere that sends you elsewhere once more.

Kinton Ramen

When I first moved to the city I went here 2x a week no problem. The one that really gets to me is the Monday special: shoyu ramen topped with juicy pieces of karaage and an egg. Thanks to these fine folks, I once won tickets to see Tampopo at TIFF. Still thankful. Watch the movie if you haven't.

Ruru Baked

New hype cream. Thankful for the friends that came through with the free drops. So blessed. So lucky. I've since had to try my luck at buying this extremely quick-selling ice cream. Third time was the charm.
