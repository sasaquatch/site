Hi there.
My name is Saša Djukić.
I help humans and technology interact. I lead the Experience & Systems Design discipline on the Razorfish FCA team, an account with some really clever and funny folk. 20 years ago I started coding, 14 years ago I learned that form follows function, been using both of those skills since.

Favourites

toronto tokyo music things moments

Imanishi

I want more endless nights at this place. Perfect izakaya food every time, and the dai dai orange sour is equally delicious.

Ramen Isshin

The Tan Tan Ramen makes me forget where I am for 20 minutes. It's a potent magical potion that will keep you alive year-round.

Dumpling House

Not sure why I always think of ordering dumplings on a Tuesday (they're closed on Tuesdays). Get the juciest fried pork and chives and forget everything that's wrong with the world with the first bite.

The island         

No cell reception in some parts and if you're facing south then it feels like you're in a different country. I never understood the haters, cuz the island to me was always a quiet space to relax and enjoy the summer sun. The sound of the waves and the sunsets are a great vibe. It's next level if you bike there, too.

North of Brooklyn

Everyone's got their pizza spot, you know something close by, something that always hits right. This place makes NY—style slices and is unpretentious about it. I like to walk around the area for an extra layer of comfy sensations.

Poetry

When returning from a trip, it's a good idea to reset at a place that has great live jazz and an atmosphere that sends you elsewhere once more.

Kinton Ramen

When I first moved to the city I went here 2x a week no problem. The one that really gets to me is the Monday special: shoyu ramen topped with juicy pieces of karaage. Thanks to these fine folks, I once won tickets to see Tampopo at TIFF. Still thankful. This is the photo is submitted.

Ruru Baked

New hype cream on the block. Thankful for the friends that came through with the free drops. I've since had to test my luck at this extremely quick-selling ice cream shop. Wasn't easy, but third time's the charm.
