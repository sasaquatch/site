52 weekends

Series of posters I made over the course of a year. Each poster celebrates the events of that week.

The style evolved because sometimes I had no time to make a poster, other times I had too much time.

I really liked the weekly cadence because it gave me a chance to step away for a few days and get a chance to miss the process.
